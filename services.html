Core Services

Everything Your Kitchen Needs

📅

Weekly Meal Planning

Tell us your dietary preferences, household size, and weekly budget — we'll generate a fully personalized meal plan with a ready-to-shop grocery list. Updated every Sunday, automatically.


🎥

Live Virtual Cooking Classes

Join interactive live sessions led by our professional chef team. Classes cover everything from knife skills and sauce fundamentals to advanced pastry techniques. Groups of 4–20 participants.


📖

Custom Recipe Collections

Our culinary team hand-curates a personal recipe collection based on your taste profile, dietary needs, and cooking skill level. Updated monthly with seasonal picks and new releases.


🍷

Wine & Food Pairing Guides

Exclusive pairing guides written by certified sommeliers and our chef team. Each guide covers varietal profiles, regional pairings, and specific bottle recommendations under $25.

Corporate Team Events

Turn your next team-building event into a memorable cooking experience. We offer virtual and in-person cooking challenges, tasting events, and culinary workshops for groups of 10–500.

Work directly with one of our professional chefs in a dedicated 60-minute session. Perfect for mastering a specific technique, troubleshooting a failing recipe, or planning a dinner party menu.
