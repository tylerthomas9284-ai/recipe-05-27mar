Our Story

Cooking Should Feel Like Home

We started Hearth & Table with one simple belief: the best meal you'll ever eat is one you made yourself, for people you love.

Who We Are

A Kitchen Built on Honesty

Hearth & Table was founded in 2016 by a group of professional chefs and passionate home cooks who were tired of recipes that looked gorgeous online but fell apart at the stove. We built something different.

Every recipe on this site is tested a minimum of three times — in home kitchens, not restaurant facilities — before it ever reaches you. If it doesn't work for a beginner cook on a Tuesday night, it doesn't get published.

We believe food is one of the most powerful ways to connect with the people around you. Our job is to make that connection as effortless and joyful as possible.

What We Stand For

Our Three Core Commitments

🧪
Rigorously Tested

Every recipe is tested at least three times in real home kitchens before publication. We track failure points, refine measurements, and rewrite until it works every time.

🌿
Ingredient Integrity

We use real, accessible ingredients — not specialty items that require a trip to three stores. If an unusual ingredient is necessary, we always provide a substitute.

👩‍🍳
Home Kitchen First

Our recipes are developed and tested on home equipment — standard ovens, basic pans, and everyday tools. No professional kitchen required.
